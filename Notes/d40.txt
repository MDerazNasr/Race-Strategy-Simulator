Added safety_car=True parameter to F1Env (env/f1_env.py):
  - SC state machine: Bernoulli trigger (prob=0.003/step when SC inactive and
    sc_cooldown=0), duration 80–200 steps, 300-step cooldown between events.
  - Speed penalty: -sc_speed_penalty × max(0, v - sc_speed_limit) per step

    Incentivises the "undercut" — pit under SC when opponent also slows.
  - Obs: sc_active (0.0/1.0) appended as last dim (dim 12 with tyre+sc → 13D).
    Synced AFTER SC state machine to avoid one-step delay.

==============================================================================
KEY FINDING: STATIC SPEED EQUILIBRIUM, NOT CONDITIONAL BEHAVIOR
==============================================================================

The zero weight on col[12] (sc_active) reveals that D40 found a STATIC speed
strategy rather than a CONDITIONAL one:

  D37 policy: ~23.67 m/s always, large SC penalties when SC fires
  D40 policy: ~21.68 m/s always, few SC penalties (avg below limit)
  Ideal:      ~26.9 m/s when green, ~22 m/s when SC (conditional on sc_active)

Why D40 settled on a static slow strategy:
  1. The SC penalty incentivised LOWERING average speed, which PPO achieved by
     training on the env where 18% of time incurs -9.8/step penalty at cv2 speed.
  2. The optimal STATIC speed (ignoring sc_active) is somewhere below 22 m/s
     to minimise expected penalty. PPO found ~21.68 m/s as its equilibrium.
  3. A CONDITIONAL strategy (fast green, slow SC) would require using col[12],
     but the static strategy gives ~zero gradient through col[12] because the
     policy doesn't need it to reduce expected penalty.
  4. Similar to D39: when a simpler strategy works, PPO doesn't bother learning
     the more complex conditional strategy.

The 57.6% SC violation rate during SC periods shows the agent is NOT
specifically slowing for SC — it just naturally averages 21.68 m/s due to its
lowered speed target. On straights it still exceeds 22 m/s, incurring penalties
it can't avoid without the sc_active signal.

This is a correct but suboptimal equilibrium: a static slow policy that
eliminates most SC penalties but sacrifices 2 laps vs D37 (13 vs 15).

FOR TRUE CONDITIONAL BEHAVIOR (WHAT WOULD CHANGE IT)
==============================================================================

To force PPO to use col[12] and learn genuine SC awareness:

Option A — Higher SC limit on green, harder to average below:
  Set sc_speed_limit=18.0 m/s (hard to always stay below → must be conditional)
  Agent can't avoid SC penalty with a static slow strategy below 18 m/s.

Option B — Green-flag speed bonus:
  Add +1.0/step bonus when v > 25 m/s AND sc_active=False.
  Creates direct incentive to drive faster on green laps → must slow on SC.

Option C — Opponent + SC (combine D39 + D40):
  Add the D39 ExpertDriver opponent at 25 m/s AND safety car events.
  Falling behind opponent (track_gap > 0) is costly, so agent must drive fast
  on green laps → pure slow strategy loses to opponent.
